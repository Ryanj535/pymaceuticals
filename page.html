Pymaceuticals

The purpose of this project was to gather, clean, sort, and visualize data from a phase 1 pharmaceutical trial
for squamous cell carcinoma. During the study, 250 mice were tested with various potential treatments over the
course of 45 days. Success was measured by tumor volume change over time, number of metastatic sites after treat-
ment, and overall lifespan of the mice after treatment.

Overall, two of the treatments were successful, having reduced metastatic sites, as well as reduced mortality rates.
Capomulin and Ramicane had volume changes of -19% and -22% respectively. Survival rates exceeded 80% over the 45 days
in comparision to less than 60% (60% being the next highest percentage, lowest being less than 30%) survival rate in
next closest treament.
